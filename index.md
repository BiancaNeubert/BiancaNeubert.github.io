I defended my Phd thesis entitled  <a href="https://archiv.ub.uni-heidelberg.de/volltextserver/37748/" target="_blank">"Nonparametric inference in convolution models"</a> supervised by  <a href="https://sip.math.uni-heidelberg.de/en/index.html" target="_blank">Jan Johannes</a> (Heidelberg University) and  <a href="https://helios2.mi.parisdescartes.fr/~comte/" target="_blank">Fabienne Comte</a> (MAP5, Université Paris Cité) on the 4th December 2025.
